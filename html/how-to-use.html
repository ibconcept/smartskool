How to Use SmartSkool

Getting Started with SmartSkool

SmartSkool provides various tools for students, teachers, and school administrators to manage and enhance the educational experience. Hereâ€™s a guide on how to use the platform based on your role.

Logging In

To access SmartSkool, log in using your credentials. You can log in as a student, teacher, or school administrator.

For Students

After logging in as a student, you can:

  • Access your class schedule and timetable.
  • View and download educational resources.
  • Engage in peer-to-peer learning through chat rooms.
  • Check your academic progress and exam results.
  • Send and receive messages from your teachers.

For Teachers

After logging in as a teacher, you will find tools such as:

  • Student Manager: View a list of classes and students, manage student records, and upload student data in Excel format.
  • Duty Roster: Access and manage the duty roster for your classes.
  • Timetable: View and manage your teaching timetable.
  • School Calendar: View important dates and events.
  • Messaging: Send messages to students and engage in chat rooms.

For School Administrators

As a school administrator (principal or headteacher), you have additional tools and capabilities:

  • Teacher and Student Management: View and monitor teacher and student accounts. Click on accounts to see activity and manage records.
  • School Calendar: Access and update the school calendar.
  • Financial Records: Update and manage financial records for the school.
  • Timetable Management: Oversee and manage the school timetable.
  • Account Management: Transfer or restore dormant accounts and manage user permissions.
  • Budget Maker: Create and manage school budgets.
  • Data Analysis: View data analysis reports to monitor school growth and performance.

Additional Features

SmartSkool offers various other features that enhance the educational experience, including:

  • Mini-Site Creation: Create and manage mini-sites for your school to store and update information.
  • Peer-to-Peer Learning: Facilitate peer-to-peer learning opportunities.
  • Assessment and Exam Tools: Manage and analyze student assessments and exam results.

Support and Assistance

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@smartskool.co.ke. We are here to help you with any questions or problems you may have.
